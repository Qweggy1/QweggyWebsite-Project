About DJ Qweggy

That frog on the internet

Hello! so you want to know more about me, not normally a fan of talking about myself but sure here we go!


I am DJ Qweggy also known as Blake to the outside world ( we dont talk about that ) i am a music producer that works within ableton live studio 11 ( A DAW ) for music production and to share my creative flair and my style of music to the world. I don't work on specific genres as of right know, i produce music from Drum & Bass to Synthwave.

As well as music production i am also a DJ currently using Beatport/My own music within virtual DJ, a well Virtual DJ deck that can be used with mouse and keyboard. This product is free to the general user however if to be used within a club or just performing in general an upgraded licence will be required!

I am currently a music producer/DJ in training it has been a pasion of mine for some time and i got the courage to stop being a lazy frog under a rock and get to learning virtual DJ and music production in hopes of making it big ( THE DREAM ) I currently stream my sets fairly regular over at my twitch would love to see you over their and interact with me.

Music Career?


When i was a tadpole i didn't actually like ANY music! Can you believe that not a single song stuck out to me. As i grew my limbs i started picking up on a lot of tunes i liked and well the dislike never came! I loved music and then it got its own little place in my tiny frog brain. I started messing around with many games and applications on both PC and Mobile for a good few years up until recently a brilliant little game came to steam called FUSER a DJ music making game where stems can be placed with other stems and it included some of todays big hits, from BLAH BLAH BLAH to KILLING IN THE NAME to LADY GAGA this is where my real music career began, i was told by many players how good i was at mixing in this game, that's where i started building an audience creating a following. Unfortunatly this game slowed down massively and it was a hard impact on me at the time. We was all in a discord group me and 130+ others we even got reconized by the main singer of OPP. Alot of us then split and the majority are still about but no where near as active. We all went seperate ways and started creating other means of music this is where i started with DAW's and DJ'in the rest is still to live today! This is where you guys are at know with me on this journey.
